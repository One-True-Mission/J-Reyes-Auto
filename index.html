About Us

10+ Years
Serving Kyle, TX

✓   Walk-Ins Welcome · No Appointment Needed

J Reyes Complete Auto was founded on one simple idea: every driver deserves honest, dependable auto care without the dealership runaround. What started as a small local shop over a decade ago has grown into a full-service repair facility trusted by families all across Kyle and the surrounding area.

Our experienced technicians combine modern diagnostic tools with old-school integrity. We take the time to explain every repair clearly, charge fair prices, and treat every vehicle like it's our own. Whether it's a routine oil change or a complex engine repair, we do the job right — the first time.

We're also set up with all major insurance providers and can handle insurance claims from start to finish. Serving Kyle, Buda, San Marcos, and the greater Hays County area.
